Our Tea Journey

From soil to sip - the story behind every cup

Rooted in Tradition

Founded with a passion for authentic flavors, Aara Foods began with a simple mission: to bring the purest tea powders from remote gardens directly to your cup. What started as a passion project between tea enthusiasts has grown into a celebration of global tea culture.

We partner directly with small-scale farmers who share our commitment to sustainable practices and exceptional quality. Each harvest is carefully selected, stone-ground, and packaged at peak freshness.
